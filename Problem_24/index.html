Movie Reviews

Inception (Sci-Fi)

Genre: Science Fiction, Thriller

Summary: A thief who steals corporate secrets through dream-sharing tech is given a chance to erase his past crimes by planting an idea into someone's mind.

Review: Brilliantly directed by Christopher Nolan, Inception blends action and mind-bending storytelling to deliver a unique cinematic experience.

Interstellar (Sci-Fi/Drama)

Genre: Sci-Fi, Adventure, Drama

Summary: In a dystopian future, a group of astronauts travel through a wormhole in search of a new home for humanity.

Review: A visually stunning and emotional space epic, Interstellar combines deep human themes with scientific imagination.

Shershaah (War/Biopic)

Genre: War, Biography, Drama

Summary: Based on the life of Captain Vikram Batra, this film showcases his courage during the Kargil War.

Review: A patriotic and emotional journey with strong performances, especially by Sidharth Malhotra.

3 Idiots (Comedy/Drama)

Genre: Comedy, Drama

Summary: Three friends navigate college life and challenge the rigid education system while pursuing true learning.

Review: Heartwarming and humorous, 3 Idiots is a classic Bollywood film that questions societal pressures and encourages innovation.
